Hi, I'm Daniil! Let me introduce myself.

I'm 22 years old. I have been working as a 3D artist and designer for almost 2 years. –êt the moment I live and work in Russia, more specifically, in Ekaterinburg where I completed my education as an architect and designer. This year I graduated from the Ural College of Construction, Architecture and Entrepreneurship.

In my spare time I often take courses in architecture and design to increase my skills, gain new experience and improve results. I have experience with the following softwares such as: 3Ds max, Corona render, Adobe Photoshop, Marvelous Designer, Unreal Engine, Arhicad, Autocad, Revit, Lumion.

I'd love to take on every project. My main preference is working with interior details. I like to combine different compositions and colors, structures and lines. On this site I present some works which I have done from the beginning until today.
